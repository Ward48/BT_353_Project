Computer Science

This page serves as a hub, connecting prospective and current Computer Science students at Stevens Institute of Technology to university and outside resources s​pecific to their major.

This website was created by 5 Stevens students studying project management. We identified a need for students to easily access extra learning materials relevant to their major. The purpose of this site is to connect Stevens students to relevant study and informational materials through a nicely organized page based on their major. Our group used AtlassianTrello to follow Agile project management with Scrum to track our progress on all the different portions of the website. This project was completed over the time period of one semester, while meeting several times a week to plan our sprints, reflect on our work, and follow the “daily standups” style for quick and efficient meetings.
