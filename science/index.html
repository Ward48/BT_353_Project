The Department of Chemistry and Chemical Biology at Stevens has a long history of innovation in education and research with excellence in laboratory instruction. The curriculum is designed to provide interdisciplinary training, rigorous theoretical instruction and exposure to experimental methods and state-of-the-art equipment. Both the Bachelor of Science in Chemistry and the Bachelor of Science in Chemical Biology are certified by the American Chemical Society. The Department of Computer Science (CS) offers two undergraduate degrees and minors: computer science and cybersecurity with undergraduate tracks in software systems and computer graphics. Computer science also offers an outstanding master’s program, with degrees in cybersecurity, machine learning, and media & broadcast engineering, in addition to a doctoral program in computer science. Eleven graduate certificates of special study are also available. The department includes world-class research centers and laboratories, including the Center for the Advancement of Secure Systems and Information Assurance (CASSIA).
